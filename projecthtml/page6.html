Madikeri or Coorg is popularly known as the 'Scotland of India'. It is one of the most beautiful hill station towns located in the southern Indian state of Karnataka. The picturesque hill station is placed on an elevation of about 1000 meters above sea level. It is renowned for its world-class coffee, and the ethnic warrior, Kodavas tribes. Coorg tour allows you to enjoy its beauty, as it has hanging valleys, deep gorges and sloppy hills, and an abundance of vegetation..


EXCITING PLACES TO VISIT

  • Abbeyfalls
  • Tadiandamol peak
  • Talakaveri

BEST TIME TO VISIT:December to February

NEAREST RAILWAY STATION:The nearest railway station from Coorg is at Mysore, about 120 km away.

Tourist Places to Visit in Uttarakhand. A land cradled in the enveloping sights of seven mountains, Nainital has always been one of the top hill stations in India. There is no escape from the mesmerizing views that nature has to offer here. The immediate draw is the crescent shapes Naini Lake from which the name Nainital has been derived. The experience of boating in the wonderful weather with enchanting sights all around is something difficult to forget.

EXCITING PLACES TO VISIT

  • Boating at Naini lake
  • Tiffin Top
  • Land ends
  • Snow View Point
  • Naina Peak

BEST TIME TO VISIT: April to June

NEAREST RAILWAY STATION: Kathgodam Railway Station is the nearest railway station, which is 24 km from Nainital
